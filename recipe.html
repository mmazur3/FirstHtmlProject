Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

This is information on how to make a peanut butter and jelly sandwich

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Two slices of Bread

Steps

  1. Place two slices of bread ona table
  2. Take a knife and scoop out peanut butter
  3. Spead peanut butter on one of the slices of bread with the knife
  4. Whipe off knife or get a new knife and scoop out jelly
  5. Use knife and spread jelly on other slice of bread
  6. Take the two slices of bread and put them together so that the peanut butter and jelly are touching
  7. Enjoy!
